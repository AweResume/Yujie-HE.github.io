Introduction

Yujie He (in Chinese: 何宇杰) is currently a senior undergraduate in pursuit of a B.Eng. Degree in mechanical engineering with specialization in mechatronics at Tongji University, Shanghai, China.

‘Diversity is essential to happiness’ by Philosopher Bertrand Russell is his life motto. Thus, he has devoted to realizing intelligent sensing & control in both industrial projects and academic researches throughout the bachelor life.

From Aug 2018, he has been working on visual tracking for unmanned aerial vehicles supervised by Dr. Changhong Fu in Vision4Robotics Group. His research interests mainly focus on the incorporation of the state-of-the-art machine learning techniques into visual perception for robotics. Besides, he is closely working with with Dr. Peng Lu from ArcLab @ HKPolyU during the undergraduate study.
