Introduction

I am currently a senior undergraduate in pursuit of a B.Eng. Degree in mechanical engineering with specialization in mechatronics.

‘Diversity is essential to happiness’ by Philosopher Bertrand Russell is my life motto. Thus, I devoted myself to realizing intelligent sensing & control in both industrial projects and academic researches during my bachelor studies.

From Aug 2018, I have started my academic exploration as an apprentice researcher in Vision4Robotics Group, Tongji University, supervised by Prof. Changhong Fu. I have a keen interest in robotic vision, with a focus on the incorporation of the state-of-the-art machine learning techniques into vision perception & processing.

Currently, I am applying for Master or PhD programs in the areas of robotics and computer vision. You can find my latest CV here.
